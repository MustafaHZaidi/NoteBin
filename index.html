notebin

what is this

Draw or write something — anything. A thought, a doodle, a secret. Crumple it up and throw it in the bin. Make it and it lives on the wall forever. Miss and it burns.

how to play

Draw on the paper. Hit Crumple & Throw to ball it up. Click and hold the ball, pull back, release to throw. You get two shots.

wind — let the elements guide you

The wind will fight you. Read the indicator before you throw.

→ ← side wind ↓ headwind — fighting you ↑ tailwind — helping you · calm

survivors

Only 15 notes can survive at a time. When the wall fills up, the oldest note is pushed out. Make yours count.
